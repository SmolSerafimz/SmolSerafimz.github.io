THE COZY REALM

CONTENTS
  • 1. OVERVIEW
  • 2. LOCATIONS

The Cozy Realm is a spiritual sanctuary where the rules of the material world such as mortality, illness, life cycle and ageing do not exist. It is a paradise where friendship is the only relation existing and happiness is the only goal. However, eternal peace has made its inhabitants the Gnomes wonder if that peace is truly perfect. If anything was missing. To answer this question a greater power has introduced a team of beings set to bring the final ingredient that such a realm need. Fun and cuteness. Their mission is introducing play, chaos, and a brand of adorable mischief that the serious old gnomes could never imagine.

This realm features many places and they will be added to the wiki as episodes introduce them.

THE COZY REALM

CONTENTS
  • 1. OVERVIEW
  • 2. LOCATIONS

The Cozy Realm is a spiritual type of a realm (which many cartoons tend to emulate by omitting negative aspects of our real material world, for obvious reasons), meaning it does not feature mortality nor lifecycles. No ageing nor physical bodies. Which means the concept of harm or illness does not exist here. Neither physical nor mental. It’s a paradise that always was like this and it will always be. Friendship is the only relation that exists and happiness is the only goal of the world itself.

But even though it is a care-free world it still has potential for even more fun and creativity. The Gnomes that inhabit this realm have wondered if their eternal peace here is truly complete. This question was answered by an intervention of a greater power which introduced a team of beings who are meant to make the world even more fun and adorable. With play and unhinged rascality they will manage what old gnomes are just too serious and too calm to introduce to the realm themselves.

This realm features many places and they will be added to the wiki as episodes introduce them.
